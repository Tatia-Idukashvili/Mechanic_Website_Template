About Us

As the official representative of Carbon Clean, Carbon Clean Georgia brings
advanced HHO and DPF cleaning technology to Tbilisi, enhancing vehicle performance and sustainability.

Using Carbon Clean’s state-of-the-art equipment, our certified technicians deliver expert
engine decarbonization and diagnostics, improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions for people.

We’re dedicated to eco-friendly practices, transparent pricing, and exceptional service. Based in Tbilisi,
we support our community with reliable carbon cleaning solutions.
